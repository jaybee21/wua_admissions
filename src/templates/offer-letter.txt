Skills for Life (Skills4Life) Courses
Under this initiative, all first-year students will complete a specialized course designed to provide practical, high-value skills. These courses are intended to empower you to generate income immediately and in the future, whether as a way to augment your finances or as a primary career path.
By integrating these practical skills into your first year of study, the University aims to ensure that every graduate possesses the entrepreneurial tools necessary for financial independence.

Students are required to choose and complete any ONE course of study.
